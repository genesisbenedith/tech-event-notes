## Official Session Description

Today's leaders are navigating rapid technological disruption, economic volatility, and both increased polarization and increasing expectations to lead with moral clarity and conviction. In this plenary, senior executives share how their values have shaped their leadership decisions, when they chose to speak publicly during challenging moments, and how they managed pushback across legal, political, and organizational environments. They also reflect on what it takes to guide teams through transformation while maintaining trust, alignment, and momentum.

**You'll gain insight into:**
- How experienced executives lead through disruption and uncertainty
- When and how leaders choose to take public positions on complex issues
- Practical lessons for sustaining trust during periods of change
