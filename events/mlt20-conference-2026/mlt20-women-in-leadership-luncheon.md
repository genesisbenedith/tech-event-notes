## Official Session Description

MLT's community contains thousands of women spanning sectors, geographies, career, and life stages. This lunch offered an opportunity to connect with other women in the MLT community and to have a candid conversation with executives and peers. Several senior executives shared lessons from their journeys, advice for women in the MLT community, and things they wish someone would have told them along the way. They discussed how they navigated the corporate world, why they chose the path they took, and what they're doing now with their power and success. They talked openly about the successes, challenges, rewards, and tradeoffs associated with their leadership journeys.

## Key Discussion Points & Notes

- **"Protect your relationships"**

- **Do your research on pay, salary, industry, market, and geographic location**
  - Jolawn relocated to Australia for a leadership role and was questioned about her salary expectations
    - They said: *"You know, you'd be the highest paid leader in this office, right?"* ???
    - *(The room collectively laughed — and it underscored exactly why doing your research matters)*
  - **Know your worth. Fight against underpay.**

- ❓⭐ *Who is going to be my accountability partner during my MBA prep and pursuit?*

- ❓⭐ *How do I deal with and navigate intimidation by others in the workplace?*

- **Give credit where it's due** — do not let other people take credit for your work

- **Think about your next pivot**
  - Rest, take care of yourself, avoid stress and burnout
  - Maintain a strengthened relationship with God

- ❓⭐ *When/what is the signal to stop investing in a workplace — and how do you tell when it's time to move on?*
  - *(Question raised by another MLT20 attendee — MBA Prep fellow/alum)*
